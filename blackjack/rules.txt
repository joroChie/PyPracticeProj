- There's a Computer Dealer and Human Player
- Player has a bank roll / Account from which we can determine maximum allowed bet and deduction/addition of earnings
- Standard 52 card deck
- Face Cards are of value 10
- Ace could be 1 or 11 depending on what's favorable to the player

- Mechanics
    - Computer Deals 2 cards to Player - both face up
    - Computer Get 1 Card Face Up and 1 Card Face down

    HUMAN TURN
        - Human Hits or Stay
            - if Human Gone beyond the sum of 21 , Dealer automatically wins
    DEALER TURN
        - Computer continues to Hit until it beats the Value Card of the Player or Goes Bust
